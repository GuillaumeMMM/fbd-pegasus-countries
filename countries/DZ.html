Algeria

In July 2021, the Pegasus Project reveals that more than 6,000 Algerian numbers were selected by Morocco as potential targets for surveillance with Pegasus. The phone numbers of former Prime Minister Noureddine Bedoui and of Minister of Foreign Affairs Ramtane Lamamra appear in the list.

In the aftermath of the Pegasus Project, Algeria’s public prosecutor orders an investigation into the allegations that the country might have been targeted with the spyware. Algeria’s Minister of Foreign Affairs Ramtane Lamamra announces that his country intends to end diplomatic relations with Morocco.
